How To Play

Mission Flow

Choose a contract in The Port, clear the mission objective, defeat the zone boss, then race the extraction window. Missing extraction still completes the contract, but only a successful extraction lets you archive a relic echo.

Loot Vacuum

Enemies spill Scrap, XP, Core Shards, and recovery cells. You have to get fairly close to pull loot in, unless a special buff or magnet effect is active.

Controls

`WASD` move, mouse aim, `LMB` fire, `Shift` dash, `Q/E/R/F` abilities, `T` deploy a tower, `C` toggle auto-fire, `Esc` pause. Abilities and towers spend Scrap during a contract.

Progression

Level up during runs to draft relics. Spend Scrap and Core Shards in the hub on permanent upgrades, classes, and new zones.

Supply Pods

Combat performance and time in-zone call down supply pods that grant repairs, loot caches, temporary overclocks, or a powerful magnet field.

Transient Relays

Some maps phase in paired teleporters for short windows. Step into one active gate to jump to the other end before the relay vanishes and relocates.

Echo Archive

Victorious runs can preserve one relic as an Echo. Archived relics automatically spin up at the start of later contracts, up to archive capacity.

Threat & Mutators

Higher threat contracts spawn stronger enemies and extra mutators, but the rewards scale sharply.
